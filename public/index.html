Designed for everyday use, tested lot by lot.

Common questions

Quick answers for buyers and dealers.

Can I request bulk quantity for multiple products?

Yes. Add products to enquiry and mention expected quantity, city, color preference and delivery requirement.

Are products made from virgin plastic?

The catalog and company profile focus on 100% virgin plastic and batch-wise quality checking.

Can the company track stock and daily sales?

Yes. The admin backend includes stock updates, stock-in entries, sold quantity records and day/month/year reports.

Where is the manufacturing base?

Manufacturing is listed at Koshi Kotwan UPSIDC area, Nabipur, Mathura, with head office in Greater Noida.
